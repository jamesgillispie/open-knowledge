# Truth Initiative Pitch

AREA 17's business-development pitch to reimagine [Truth Initiative](/clients/truth-initiative/overview.md)'s digital ecosystem as a single orchestration layer rather than a collection of disconnected websites. The final, in-person presentation was delivered to Truth Initiative's C-suite and org-wide stakeholders in Washington, DC on **April 8, 2026**.

## Status

Pitch delivered (DC, April 8). Originally slated for late February, the presentation slipped twice before the April 8 date. Awaiting outcome.

## Why it matters

Truth Initiative's properties are fragmented: truthinitiative.org and thetruth.com both run on Drupal (sharing infrastructure but not actually connected), the EX Program runs on a separate Wagtail stack tied to user data, and thetruth.com additionally carries an expensive Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) license. The pitch's central reframe is that this is **"not a website, it's an ecosystem"** — a unified digital core with a shared governance model and integrated data architecture. The proof point is AREA 17's National Academies replatforming, where discoverability work drove a ~1,400% Search Console increase.

## What the pitch proposed

- A reframed opportunity narrative: psychographics over demographics, AI as another audience/user group, dynamic (generative) design systems — see [Generative / Data-Driven Design Systems](/concepts/generative-design-systems.md) and [AEO/GEO & AI as a Site Visitor](/concepts/aeo-geo.md).
- A future-ready platform foundation (Laravel 12 recommended over opinionated Drupal; recommend canceling the costly AEM license), with interoperability framed as "building a house vs. renting a house."
- Four experience modes / audience tracks (advocate / influence / quit / support) with per-audience personalization, and storytelling as the organization-wide theme — uplifting the stories of those Truth serves (AGs, older smokers, the Juul generation, activists), with the EX Community forum as a rich content asset.
- Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) alongside traditional SEO, anchored by an AI-generated SEO audit of truthinitiative.org.

## Key decisions

- **Move technical architecture and schematics to the appendix** — keep only the multi-site / Schroeder Institute fit diagram in the main deck. Tech detail was deemed too reductive for a CEO-level audience; Mickey is the only stakeholder who cares about the specifics.
- **Lead with the National Academies case study** — a large-scale replatforming/ecosystem revamp directly analogous to Truth's situation.
- **Reframe the close** around ecosystem/orchestration rather than a website build.
- **Offer a [Mickey](/people/mickey.md) technical follow-up** for the one stakeholder who wants depth.
- **Lead with Truth Initiative and thetruth.com** as the primary creative brief; acknowledge the EX Program's consolidation upside but do not over-index on it (it recently launched on a separate platform and is mid-transition under a new partnership).

## Format and logistics

Run as a live, animated Figma prototype (no PDF fallback), requiring an internet connection. In-person from Truth Initiative: Trisha, Mickey, Caitlin, plus one other. [Nikki](/people/nikki.md) joined virtually as product owner.

## Presentation structure

Intro → AREA 17 overview → digital transformation philosophy → case study → creative concepts / ideation (the bulk, ~30–40 min).
