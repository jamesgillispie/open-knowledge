# AREA 17

AREA 17 is a global brand, experience, and technology agency founded in 2003, with studios in New York and Paris. It is [James Gillispie](/people/james-gillispie.md)'s employer, where he serves as Engineering / Technical Director.

## What it is

- A Laravel / PHP shop. AREA 17 builds and maintains the open-source Twill CMS, which underpins much of its client work.
- Known for cultural-institution, archive, and editorially rich work.
- Technology is its largest team — engineering capacity is central to how the agency wins and delivers.

## 2026 transformation

AREA 17 is undergoing a [transformation from skills-based groups to outcome-based pods](/projects/a17-transformation.md) — reorganizing the agency around cross-disciplinary teams that own outcomes rather than disciplines that own crafts. This sits alongside a broader push on [AI tooling and internal agents](/projects/ai-tooling-initiative.md), and surfaces a recurring tension around [director overload and the IC-vs-strategist balance](/concepts/director-overload.md).
