# Sprint 15 Planning and Year-End Review with Anthony

### TRV Platform Status

- L3 pages mostly complete, missing events and news
- Events not yet pulled in - incomplete data, awaiting client confirmation
    - Can associate events to committees
    - Parent unit events showing on level two
- Migration scripts for committee members from Appdify ready for production
- News source unclear due to missing budget ID

### Infrastructure & Performance Issues

- Node one architecture problems causing CPU bottlenecks
    - Handles OpenSearch, PHP, gets hammered by traffic
    - Other nodes underutilized (database only)
- Redis cache move off node one could provide significant improvement
    - Follow-up email sent to hosting team (Charles back from leave)
- Bad actor IP crawls from China causing site instability
- Upsun proactively flagging and blocking malicious traffic
- Server resources doubled/tripled during launch - need to optimize back down

### Technology Optimization Discussion

- Blade vs Boost evaluation for Livewire components
    - Blaze is newer, Anthony more familiar with structure
    - Boost compatibility unknown
    - Could provide significant performance gains for simple components
- Tailwind merge optimizations pushed yesterday, running stable
- Redis cache relocation priority for 2026

### Holiday Coverage & Handover

- Anthony’s last day Friday, limited availability next year
    - One full week potentially January
    - 4 hours/week for care duties
- Yinka available during break (no other projects)
    - Added as admin to production/staging/development on Upsun
    - Needs CLI tools documentation review
    - Will handle emergency monitoring/alerts
- Care team not yet onboarded - can’t handle emergencies during holidays

### Sprint 15 Planning & Backlog

- 100+ tickets moved to Sprint 15 for January start
- 6-week development period allocated from 3-year NASAM budget
    - Full-time developer + TD support + product owner + QA
- High complexity items flagged for separate planning:
    - Permalink restructuring (requires workshop, proper sprint)
    - Newsletter preferences integration with BigQuery/HubSpot
    - Breadcrumb functionality
- Redirect management tool needed for Twill (Laravel capsule available)
- SSO integration ticket needs reopening

### Next Steps

- Close/move remaining MVP launch tickets to Sprint 15
- Reset staging/dev environments early 2026
- Proper complexity estimation session needed for backlog prioritization
- Upsun relationship evaluation - trust degraded but stability improving
