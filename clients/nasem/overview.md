# National Academies (NASEM)

The National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine (NASEM) is an [AREA 17](/clients/area-17/overview.md) client. Auto-transcripts render the name as "NASAM" / "Nascent"; the Obsidian source uses the `nasem` slug.

## What it is

A Twill-based site now in a **sprint-based care / enhancement phase**, centered on a complex OpenSearch-driven platform with extreme interdependencies — changes in one area ripple widely, which is why work is scoped into tightly bounded sprints rather than a continuous retainer (see [Sprint vs. Retainer](/concepts/sprint-vs-retainer.md)).

## Engagement summary

- Roughly **$80k of 2027 care budget remaining**, framed as two six-week sprints.
- Also serves as one of AREA 17's flagship case studies (Truth Initiative-adjacent in pitch usage).

## Active workstreams

- Multi-track opportunities / awardees tabs
- URL restructuring
- Betty AI sitemap integration
- Flagship events, notably TRB
- Bot mitigation (see [Bot Management / Edge & SEO](/concepts/bot-management-seo.md))
- Large [multi-branch releases](/concepts/release-engineering.md) — e.g. Growth 28.01
