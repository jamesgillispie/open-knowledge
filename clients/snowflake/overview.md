# Snowflake

Snowflake is a data-platform vendor evaluated for AREA 17's agency analytics needs. It supports OLTP and OLAP natively (via its Crunchy Data Postgres acquisition) and offers native vector retrieval, positioning it as a single platform for transactional, analytical, and AI-retrieval workloads.

## Why it matters

Snowflake was assessed on a joint call alongside [Pliable](/clients/pliable/overview.md), whose analytics product builds its semantic layer entirely inside Snowflake (keeping client data from egressing to external LLMs). In one note, Snowflake was ruled out for AREA 17's specific needs — its strength as a heavy data platform exceeded what the agency's analytics use case required.
