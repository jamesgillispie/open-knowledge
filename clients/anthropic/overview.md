# Anthropic (Claude)

Anthropic is the LLM provider whose tools AREA 17 uses across both delivery and operations. It is central to the agency's [AI Tooling / Internal Agent Initiative](/projects/ai-tooling-initiative.md).

## How AREA 17 uses it

- **Claude Opus** for release-branch regression audits — auditing large release branches for regressions before merging (notably on the [NASEM Site](/projects/nasem-site.md)), establishing AI regression auditing in the release process.
- **Claude Sonnet** for writing.
- **Claude Tag Slack integration**, trialed with $2,500 in free credits, as the basis for proposed business-development automation.

## Why it matters

Claude tooling spans the full delivery-to-ops spectrum: engineering QA, content writing, and BD automation. This breadth makes Anthropic the anchor vendor for AREA 17's internal AI adoption push.
