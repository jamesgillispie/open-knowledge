# Jane Street

Jane Street is a quantitative trading firm with an applicant-first recruitment brand, and an [AREA 17](/clients/area-17/overview.md) client. Auto-transcripts spell it "James Street"; the source folder is `Jane Street`.

## What it is

AREA 17 is [redesigning Jane Street's website](/projects/jane-street-redesign.md) around a **data-driven generative visual identity** — built on Wolfram rules / cellular automata — paired with contextual and research-oriented prompts. See [Generative / Data-Driven Design Systems](/concepts/generative-design-systems.md).

## Stack and timeline

- [Payload CMS](/clients/payload/overview.md) + Next.js.
- Supported through an **October 2026 launch**.

## Why it matters

- A candidate for [AI personalization / AEO-GEO](/concepts/aeo-geo.md) — first-party LinkedIn data driving an adaptive UI.
- The **strongest case study for the [Millennium Management](/clients/millennium-management/overview.md) pitch**: no conflict, with close applicant-first parallels.
