# Winston Taylor (Project Meridian)

Winston Taylor is a newly merged international law firm — the combination of Winston & Strawn and Taylor Wessing — and an [AREA 17](/clients/area-17/overview.md) client. The internal codename is **Project Meridian**.

## What it is

AREA 17 is building an [MVP website](/projects/winston-taylor-mvp.md) anchored by a large insights / news / events section — roughly **1,053 items to migrate** (see [Content Migration, Taxonomy & CMS Templating](/concepts/content-migration-taxonomy.md)).

## Stack

- A Twill-adjacent build.
- [Meilisearch](/clients/meilisearch/overview.md) for site search.
- Formie forms.
- Dual CRMs.
- OneTrust consent management (CMP).
- [Foleon](/clients/foleon/overview.md) for gated content.

## Timeline

- Functional site targeted for **end of May**.
- Content and gated reports by **June 1**.
