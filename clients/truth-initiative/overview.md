# Truth Initiative

Truth Initiative is an anti-tobacco / anti-nicotine public health nonprofit and an [AREA 17](/clients/area-17/overview.md) prospect. Its properties include truthinitiative.org, thetruth.com, the EX Program, and the Schroeder Institute.

## What it is

A mission-driven nonprofit where storytelling and fundraising are central. It currently runs on **two Drupal instances**.

## The pitch

AREA 17 [pitched a digital transformation](/projects/truth-initiative-pitch.md) — final in-person pitch in DC on **April 8** — framed around:

- **Four experience modes:** advocate, influence, quit, support.
- **Four transformation principles** guiding the rebuild.
