# Meilisearch

Meilisearch is a French open-source search engine (an Algolia competitor) used by [AREA 17](/clients/area-17/overview.md) as a vendor.

## What they are

- Open-source search, positioned against Algolia.
- **Pricing model:** document / server-size based (t-shirt sizing), with unlimited searches.

## How AREA 17 uses it

AREA 17 stood up **Meilisearch Cloud for [Winston Taylor](/clients/winston-taylor/overview.md) search**, reusing AREA 17's existing GCF (Green Climate Fund) account. See [Search Platform Evaluation & Relevance](/concepts/search-relevance.md).
