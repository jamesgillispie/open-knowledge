# Getty (Pacific Standard Time / PST 2030)

Getty's Pacific Standard Time (PST) is a grant program funding roughly **100 LA cultural institutions** for California-art exhibitions. Getty is an [AREA 17](/clients/area-17/overview.md) prospect, building PST as a **persistent brand and digital home for 2030** rather than a one-off campaign site.

## The proposal

A roughly **$125K proposal** covering:

- A public site.
- A partner portal (~100 grantee logins).
- A narrative archive.
- CMS + CRM as supporting infrastructure (not the headline).
