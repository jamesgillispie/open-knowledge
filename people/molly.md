# Molly

Client-side project lead at [Jane Street](/clients/jane-street/overview.md).

## Role

Molly is the primary AREA 17 contact and technical program manager on the Jane Street website redesign — the person the team routes design decisions, prompt strategy, and approvals through, and who handles coordination on the client side.

## What she does

- Serves as the lead contact for design and prompt decisions and approvals.
- Coordinates workshop scheduling and project status, and routes copy/content decisions.
- Owns client-side logistics such as Google Cloud search endpoint/access questions.

## Context

Molly is candid in reviews (she once volunteered to "be the downer" and called a direction "extremely boring"), which makes her a useful taste check during the collaborative design sprints. She is the key approver for the [Jane Street website redesign](/projects/jane-street-redesign.md), where AREA 17 runs an embedded sprint model with Jane Street's internal design team. Her work touches the [generative / data-driven design systems](/concepts/generative-design-systems.md) theme central to that engagement.
