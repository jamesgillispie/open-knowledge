# Stacy (Stacey)

Client-side product / content stakeholder on the [National Academies (NASEM)](/clients/nasem/overview.md) engagement. (Appears in notes as both "Stacy" and "Stacey Andolia.")

## Role

Stacy's team manages NASEM's flagship events, including the TRB annual meeting. She is one of the two main client leads (with [Talia](/people/talia.md)) juggling tickets across every facet of the website and QAing items in staging ahead of deployment.

## What she does

- Sent the "red alert" email flagging that site search was not meeting stakeholder expectations.
- Forwards ready-to-deploy tickets mid-release and has priority tickets she pushes into discussion.
- Tends to add scope to existing tickets rather than creating new ones — a habit the team is actively correcting.
- Is adapting (with the agency's help) from an emergency-patch request style to a structured release schedule.

## Context

Stacy has a reputation on the project for running hot — described as habitually "panicky" across three years of the relationship — which makes the move to a predictable release cadence especially valuable. She and Talia did a full backlog cleanup and reprioritization together. Her search complaint is a concrete driver of the [search platform evaluation & relevance](/concepts/search-relevance.md) work, and her ticket habits feed the [margin / over-servicing discipline](/concepts/margin-over-servicing.md) and [content migration & taxonomy](/concepts/content-migration-taxonomy.md) themes on the [NASEM site](/projects/nasem-site.md).
