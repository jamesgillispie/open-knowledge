# Mike

**Role:** Experience discipline lead at [AREA 17](/clients/area-17/overview.md).

Mike is responsible for resetting the Experience methodology and principles across studios — defining how the discipline works and what good looks like.

## What he does

- **Experience methodology.** Owns and is actively resetting the Experience discipline's principles and practices firm-wide.
- **Client framing.** On the [Jane Street redesign](/projects/jane-street-redesign.md) SteerCo, Mike opened the presentation by reflecting the client's round-one feedback back to them — that outputs shouldn't feel decorative and must reflect who Jane Street really is — before handing to [Tucker](/people/tucker.md) to walk through the data-viz framework.
- **Business development.** Attends [Getty PST](/clients/getty-pst/overview.md) BD calls and weighs in on the proposal approach.

## Context

Mike operates at the strategy/experience altitude — keeping work "at 5,000 feet," intentional and meaningful, rather than dropping prematurely into colors and ground-level execution.
