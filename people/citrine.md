# Citrine

Associate director at [AREA 17](/clients/area-17/overview.md), promoted in Q2 2026 to lead the global new-business pipeline.

## Role

Citrine owns the front end of business development. When an inbound lead arrives, she is the one who, with the accounts team, validates whether it is responsive and worth pursuing — and announces when a qualified inbound has been accepted, which kicks off team planning. Going forward she creates the project record for every new response, so that every lead has a named owner.

## What she does

- Leads and manages the global BD pipeline; partners with [Mark](/people/mark.md) on New York opportunities.
- Runs the qualification gate with the accounts team and announces accepted inbounds.
- Ensures inbound leads have named owners and that projects are created for all new responses.

## Context

Citrine has carried some of the firm's longest sales cycles (one client trailed roughly five years before converting). She travels frequently between offices (often in Europe), and is flagged as the governance / ops check-in contact for any BD automation work — the human owner James would route a pipeline tool through. See [FTZ Analytics / GTM Program](/projects/ftz-analytics.md) and the broader [AI tooling initiative](/projects/ai-tooling-initiative.md) for the automation context.
