# Jasmine

Head of Product for Nexus at [Pinecone](/clients/pinecone/overview.md), roughly four weeks into the company at the time of the evaluation.

## Role

Jasmine leads Pinecone's Nexus product — the system that compiles raw data into structured "knowledge artifacts" via a curation runtime and then queries them via NoQL (a declarative query language that lets agents specify output shape, grounding, budget, and scope). She ran the Nexus / NoQL demo for James and committed to provisioning a dedicated test environment.

## What she does

- Ran the Nexus/NoQL demo, walking through the two runtimes: Curate (ingest + design manifest) and Query (NoQL-driven retrieval with citations).
- Is provisioning a dedicated test environment and preview key for James to explore ingestion and querying firsthand.
- Flagged a candid product boundary: real-time website personalization may be better served by a vector DB directly than by Nexus.

## Context

Jasmine was James's main product counterpart in the [Pinecone Nexus evaluation](/projects/pinecone-nexus-eval.md), alongside account manager Lucas Angstrom and VP of Marketing Aaron. The eval connects to James's interest in [RAG 2.0 / structured knowledge retrieval](/concepts/rag-structured-retrieval.md) and [search relevance](/concepts/search-relevance.md).
