# Talia

Client point person on the [National Academies (NASEM)](/clients/nasem/overview.md) engagement.

## Role

Talia is one of AREA 17's primary client-side contacts on NASEM, working between the ITS / development team and the agency. She coordinates future-sprint scope and surfaces the operational risks — particularly the backlog of tickets assigned to her for review that block deployment.

## What she does

- Flags unreviewed-ticket risk (tickets assigned to her for review that ultimately gate releases) and acknowledges when some will linger past a deadline.
- Coordinates future-sprint scope and the cleanout / reprioritization of the backlog with [Stacy](/people/stacy.md).
- Was the contact looped in on the Fastly-direct vs. Cloudflare hosting question (with [Mark](/people/mark.md) brought in first).

## Context

Talia and Stacy plan around each other's PTO to keep continuity on the project, and discussed how to proceed through coverage periods. Her ticket-review bottleneck is a recurring driver of the [environment hygiene / pre-prod & QA workflow](/concepts/environment-hygiene.md) and [release engineering](/concepts/release-engineering.md) concerns on the [NASEM site](/projects/nasem-site.md).
