# Miguel (Mozzie)

**Role:** Designer / creative technologist at [AREA 17](/clients/area-17/overview.md); lead designer on the [Jane Street redesign](/projects/jane-street-redesign.md).

Miguel leads design on Jane Street and sits at the intersection of design and code — he vibe-codes prototypes and hand-built the generative tooling behind the design directions.

## What he does

- **Generative pattern / canvas generators.** Hand-coded the pattern and canvas generators driving the Jane Street design system — using Wolfram rules and cellular automata to produce data-driven, meaningful (not decorative) visual output. See [generative / data-driven design systems](/concepts/generative-design-systems.md).
- **Design directions.** Presented the four design directions to the Jane Street SteerCo.
- **Workshop leadership.** Wrote the brief for the collaborative sketching kickoff with the client (Steven and Ryan) to define the "Jane Street DNA" components.

## Context

Miguel works closely with [Britton](/people/britton.md) on the creative-technology side of Jane Street, and with [Tucker](/people/tucker.md) and [Mike](/people/mike.md) on framing the design system for the client.
